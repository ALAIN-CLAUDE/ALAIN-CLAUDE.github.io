Lightning Flow

A flow is the part of Lightning Flow that collects data and performs actions in your Salesforce org or an external system. Types of Lightning Flow are:

  • screen flows
  • autolaunched flows
  • Scheduled Triggered Flow
  • Record Triggered Flow
  • Platform Triggered Event flow

  4. Loop over those contact records .


  5. Create a record variable and Assign these Looped.


  6. Create another record variable and finally Add all records from step 5 .


  7. Create contact roles for the newly created Opportunity with contacts from opportunity's account .


  8. Activate and Test
