Approval Process In Salesforce

An approval process automates how records are approved in Salesforce. An approval process specifies each step (initial submission action,approval steps, final approval action,final rejection action and recall action) of approval, including from whom to request approval and what to do at each point of the process..

Leave Approval Request Setup

A Senior nurse Julia Makaron plans to go on vacation but will require an approval from her Manager Mari Anita. This is how you Create it the approval

Go to setup and type approval process and click on it. Select the object Under manage Approval process for . In our case choose Leave Requests object. To create choose Use Standard Setup Wizard . with Standard setup you can configure all you need while with Use Jump start Wizard its direct and few steps. Name it "Leave Approval Process" and click next.

We wont require a criteria on this so click next again. Under Next automated approval choose Manager and choose Administrator ONLY can edit record which is the option we are using in this case and click next and next.

Select all fields execpt Last modified and click on next.these fields will be available on the page layout of the request.

On initial submitter choose only the leave request owner as in our case but you may choose all users depending on your requirement. click on save. choose i'll do this later.Take me to the approval deatil page to review what i've just created.

Next we need to create our approval steps. to put in place who the approval should be send to.

name it.

click on new approval step . --Allow all records should enter this step and click on next. --choose Automatically assign using the user field selected earlier(manager).

we could choose first option if we need user to choose who will be the appover at time of submission and last option if we need both manager and admin to accept before approval goes through(unanimous) or either of them. Click next. and choose no.

Now Julia Makaron can create a request

Maria can accept Julia's the request when she login or receive an email notification as well
